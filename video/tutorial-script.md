## Production Notes

### Voiceover Guidelines
- **Tone:** Professional, confident, solution-oriented
- **Pace:** ~2.5 words per second (moderate pace)
- **Voice:** Clear, engaging, neutral accent (American or British English)
- **Emphasis:** 
  - "BAGANA AI" (brand name)
  - "one unified workflow"
  - "without increasing your manual workload"

### Music & Sound
- **Background music:** Upbeat, professional, tech-forward (instrumental)
- **Volume:** Low enough not to compete with narration
- **Sound effects:** Subtle UI interaction sounds (optional)

### Text Overlays
- Key phrases highlighted on screen:
  - "One Platform"
  - "Multi-Agent System"
  - "Save Hours"
  - "Data-Driven Decisions"
- Website URL at end: bagana-ai.com (or GitHub link)

### Technical Specifications
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD) minimum
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (YouTube/LinkedIn standard)
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Audio:** Stereo, 48kHz
