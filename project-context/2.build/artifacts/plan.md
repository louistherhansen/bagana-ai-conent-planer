# Overview
The campaign aims to promote Silverqueen chocolate through engaging content featuring Deddy Corbuzier. This will harness Deddy's influence to create a strong connection between the brand and the audience, enhancing brand recognition and sales.

# Objectives
1. Increase brand awareness of Silverqueen chocolate among millennials.
2. Drive engagement through creative content that resonates with Deddy's followers.
3. Boost sales of Silverqueen chocolate by 20% within the campaign duration.

# Talent Assignments
- **Deddy Corbuzier** - Lead talent for all major content pieces, including video, social media posts, and live events.
- **Supporting Creators**: Identify additional influencers for social media amplification.

# Content Themes
1. **Behind the Scenes**: Show Deddy's process of enjoying and preparing recipes with Silverqueen.
2. **Challenges**: Create fun engagement challenges involving Silverqueen.
3. **Testimonials**: Feature Deddy's personal stories and experiences connected to the product.

# Timeline
- **Week 1**: Kick-off meeting and content brainstorming session.
- **Week 2**: Production of video content and creation of social media posts.
- **Week 3**: Soft launch of content, start of engagement challenges.
- **Week 4-5**: Full campaign launch; major promotional events by Deddy.
- **Week 6**: Campaign evaluation and feedback collection.

# Key Messaging
- "Coklat Silverqueen: Lebih dari sekedar coklat, ini adalah pengalaman."
- Call-to-action: "Ikuti kami di media sosial dan bagikan momen manismu!"

# Audit
The content will be audited weekly for alignment with objectives and relevance to the target audience. Adjustments will be made based on engagement metrics and feedback.
